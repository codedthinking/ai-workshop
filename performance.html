The unbundling point

The same task that costs $4.80 on Opus costs $0.96 on Sonnet and $0.08 on DeepSeek V3. The quality differs — but for many research tasks, the cheaper model is good enough. The point of the unbundling argument: pick the model per task, not per month. Use Opus for the hard architecture decisions, Sonnet for daily work, and a cheap model for bulk formatting.
